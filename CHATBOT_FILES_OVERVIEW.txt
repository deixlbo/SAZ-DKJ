HOW TO USE:
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

FOR END USERS:
  1. Click green chat bubble (bottom-right)
  2. Type question OR click microphone to speak
  3. Read AI response
  4. Click copy icon to save important info
  5. Use quick prompts for instant answers

FOR DEVELOPERS:
  1. Read README_CHATBOT.md for architecture
  2. Check chatbot-config.ts for configuration
  3. Use chatbot-utils.ts for helper functions
  4. Customize CHATBOT_CONFIG for your needs
  5. Connect to Supabase for real data
