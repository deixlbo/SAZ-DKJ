## 📱 MOBILE APP SUPPORT

Your app works as a Progressive Web App (PWA):

**Android Installation:**
1. Open in Chrome
2. Menu (⋮) → Install app
3. App appears on home screen

**iOS Installation:**
1. Open in Safari
2. Share (↗) → Add to Home Screen
3. App appears on home screen

**Features:**
- Works offline
- Installable
- App shortcuts
- Full screen mode
